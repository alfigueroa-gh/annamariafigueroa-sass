Band Members Biography

"Harvey Dale and the Cements, a hard-hitting punk/electronic four-piece from Austin, Texas, combines an unleashed live performance with edgy lyrics, unforgettable hooks and well-studied fuzz guitar sounds. A must-see, must-hear new band." -Screecher Magazine

Harvey Dale

Harvey was born in California and grew up loving music. He is one of the lead members of Harvey Dale and The Cements.

Patrick Cement

Patrick was born in Rhode Island and grew up playing the guitar. His parents were musicians as well. He is the lead guitarist of Harvey Dale and The Cements.

Henry Cement

Henry was born in Maine and did not become interested in music until his senior year of high school. He joined the school band and fell in love with music. He plays the keys in the band.
